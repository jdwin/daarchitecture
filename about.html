David Anderson - About Me

I chose to pursue studies in architecture because I am fascinated by the idea of creating, inventing, and building. I am drawn to the study of human interactions within public and private environments.

In 2013, I joined the Azrieli School of Architecture at Carleton University (Ottawa) after finishing a cégep diploma in Pure and Applied Science (Sherbrooke, Québec).

As I continue to travel abroad, I expand my understanding of different places and cultures, as well as indulge my passion for architecture, art, and photography!
